Exodus Unfinished:
A Haggadah for a World Still in Chains

Rabbi Barry Dolinger and Naomi Baine

The Rabbis insisted that the Exodus is not just a memory, it is a calling. A command to see ourselves as if we personally left slavery, and to recognize the chains that still bind the world. Through attentive commentary and spiritually attuned reflection, Rabbi Barry Dolinger and Naomi Baine illuminate the timeless wisdom of the Haggadah while inviting readers to confront one uncomfortable truth: the work of liberation did not end in Egypt. Today, millions remain trapped in forced labor and human trafficking. The Exodus story asks what it would mean to continue on the journey our ancestors began. Because the Exodus is not only a story we tell. It is a story we are still writing.
